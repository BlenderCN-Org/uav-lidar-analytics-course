Flight plan preparation

GIS595/MEA792: UAV/lidar Data Analytics

NCSU OSGeoREL at Center for Geospatial Analytics

Objectives

  • Understand the importance of flight planning for safe mission execution
  • Recognize the relationship between flight parameters and acquired data quality
  • Choose appropriate mission area and flight parameters for answering previously stated research question
  • Outline the workflow for flight planning - in the lab and in the field
  • Analyze flight path taking into account terrain obstacles, UAS technical limitations and legal issues
  • Prepare ready-to-use flight plan for executing the mission

Where yould you like to fly?

In this assigment you need to justify the choice of the flight area and prove that the geographical and legal conditions allowing for a save flight execution.

Why do you want to fly there?

Considering the technical, meteorological and legal conditions of the possible flight area choice, you shouldn’t forget about the most important element: the aim and purpose of the flight.

Show us your flight idea!

You will present your idea to the collegues, so try to find an interesting research problem.

  • The presentation needs to be short (2-3min) and doesn’t need to include all the information from the assignment.

Written version of the assignment is based on two tasks: Flight planning in Trimble Aerial Imaging and GIS analysis of flight plan using lidar-based DSM

Report should include:

  1. Motivation for choosing the flight area
  2. Relief conditions and legal issues
  3. Flight parameters adn specifications
  4. Remarks regarding the workflow (any problems encoutered? difficultes? suggestions? new solutions?)
  5. Attachments
    • Map of the flight area
    • Flight plan file (.gwt files)
    • Presentation file

1. Motivation

State the research problem

    Why do you want to fly over this area?
    • what problem are you aiming to solve,
    • what research question to answer,
    • or what object or phenomenon to investigate.

2. Relief conditions and legal issues

  • Brief description of geographical conditions (topography, land use, terrain obstacles, ground activity etc.)
  • Property of the ground (public, private - is there permission from the landowner?)
  • Formal possibilities of flight execution (does this area have COA, who obtained it, what is the status of the area according to the aeronautical charts

3. Flight parameters adn specifications

  • Targeted area (ha or sq. km)
  • Area radius (distance between the ground station location and the furthest spot to be mapped
  • Chosen take off, landing and alternate landing location (short description helping with localization of the spots)
  • Accessibility of the take-off location (brief description of possible obstacles or difficulties to get to the area or set the launcher)
  • Elevation differences between the Digital Surface Model and the flight ceiling (in GIS)
  • Edesired resolution and chosen overlap

4. Remarks regarding the workflow

Show us your work!

  • Maps and photos highly recommended
  • Did you encoutered any problems with the software?
  • Did you have any difficulties, eureka moments?

upload files to Moodle

    • Take off and landing locations
    • Safe, obstacle free take off and landing
    • Borders of the desired mapping area
  • Flight plan file (.gwt, .kml and/or other files in the software of your choice)
  • Presentation file
