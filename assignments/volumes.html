Volume and area measurements in Agisoft Photoscan

Task:

Based on the imagery acquired 07/02/2017 at Wake Waste Transfer calculate the volume and area of the "dirt pile" (check the location)

Data:

Imagery available here

Tips

  • follow the Agisoft Photoscann tutorial on volume measurements
  • the delimitation of the file (mask applied) is up to you - you can examine whatever part of the pile wyou ant to process
  • use a high (or custom) face count setting in mesh generation to have detailed mesh
  • remember to close holes (level 100%)
  • you can additionally generate contours
