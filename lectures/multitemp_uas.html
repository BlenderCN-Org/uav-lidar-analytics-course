UAS for monitoring changes

Low cost and rapid deployment: excellent for monitoring changes at local scale (fields, small watersheds)
  • crop monitoring: growth, disease, stress
  • erosion processes: coastal, stream bank, overal flow
  • natural disasters: flooding, landslides, fire
  • mining, construction sites

Monitoring design

  • size of the area, spatial resolution needed to capture the changes
  • temporal resolution: regular intervals, event driven mapping
  • georeferencing, rectification: GCPs distribution and survey

Processing time series

  • use GCPs and permanent features to evaluate accuracy
  • check and correct systematic errors (horizontal, vertical) if needed
